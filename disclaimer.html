Legal & Risk Disclosures

Trust ZedFund is designed to support community savings, education, and local ventures. We prioritize transparency, education, and responsible participation. Please read the following carefully.

Non-Bank Platform

Trust ZedFund is not a bank or a licensed financial institution. We do not hold or manage your funds directly in a bank account. All member contributions are tracked in platform accounts for learning and illustration purposes.

No Guaranteed Returns

Any illustrations of project growth, community contributions, or returns are examples only. There are no guaranteed profits. All contributions are voluntary and at your own risk.

Voluntary Participation

Members choose if and when to contribute. Participation in Trust Circles or community projects is optional. There are no forced payments or automatic debits.

Member Responsibility

You are responsible for understanding your contributions and decisions. We provide education, tracking, and illustrations, but you must make informed choices.

Illustrative Data

Charts, dashboards, and simulations are designed for learning, motivation, and transparency. They do not reflect real investment guarantees or performance.

Regulatory Status

Trust ZedFund operates under PACRA registration as a financial service activity. We are not licensed by the Bank of Zambia or SEC Zambia to offer banking, deposit-taking, or investment guarantees.

By using Trust ZedFund, you acknowledge these disclosures and accept responsibility for your participation.
