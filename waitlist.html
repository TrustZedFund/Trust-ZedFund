Frequently Asked Questions

When will Trust ZedFund launch?

We're launching in phases starting Q4 2024 in Lusaka, with provincial expansion through 2025. Waitlist members get priority access in their province.

Is there any cost to join the waitlist?

No, joining the waitlist is completely free. There's no obligation to use Trust ZedFund when we launch.

What information do you collect?

Just your name, email, phone number, and province. We use this to notify you when we launch in your area and for verification purposes.

Can I join if I'm not in Lusaka?

Yes! Join now and we'll notify you when we expand to your province. The earlier you join, the higher your priority for early access.
