Frequently Asked Questions

Is my money safe in a savings circle?

Yes! Funds are held in secure escrow accounts with our licensed mobile money partners. Each transaction is recorded and transparent to all circle members. We never hold funds directly in Trust ZedFund accounts.

What happens if someone doesn't pay?

Each circle sets its own rules for late payments. Common approaches include small penalties, temporary suspension, or requiring guarantors. The social accountability within trusted circles keeps compliance high.

How are returns generated?

Returns come from two sources: 1) Interest charged when circle members borrow from the pool, and 2) Investment of idle funds in our Community Pools which fund Zambian businesses at higher returns.

Can I withdraw my money anytime?

Withdrawal rules are set by each circle. Most circles allow withdrawals with notice (e.g., 1-2 weeks) and may require paying any outstanding loans first. Emergency withdrawals are typically accommodated.
