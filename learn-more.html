Trust ZedFund

Trust ZedFund is a community-focused financial platform for Zambians. We exist to help individuals and communities understand money, save together responsibly, and support real projects — without being a bank, without promising returns, and without forcing participation.

Important: If you are looking for guaranteed profits, fast money, or someone to invest on your behalf, Trust ZedFund is not for you.

Who This Platform Is For

Trust ZedFund is designed for:

  • Individuals starting with small amounts (K100–K500)
  • People who want to learn before risking money
  • Communities that want transparency and structure
  • Users who prefer clarity over promises

Trust ZedFund is not for:

  • People seeking guaranteed or fixed returns
  • People who want passive income without involvement
  • People uncomfortable with real-world risk

What Trust ZedFund Does (And Does Not Do)

What We Do

  • Provide financial education and learning tools
  • Show how savings circles and community projects work
  • Offer transparency, records, and tracking systems
  • Help members make informed, voluntary decisions

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not take deposits like a bank
  • We do not invest money on your behalf
  • We do not guarantee profits or outcomes
  • We do not pressure anyone to contribute funds

Learner / Observer Membership

This membership is for individuals who want to understand how collective saving and community projects work before participating financially.

The membership fee exists because Trust ZedFund is a serious platform. It supports education, record-keeping, transparency tools, and platform operations. There are no free trials or test accounts.

What You Receive

  • Structured financial education
  • Real examples and case studies
  • View-only access to how groups operate
  • Simulated scenarios and learning tools
  • Time to decide without pressure

What You Do Not Receive

  • No access to pooled funds
  • No investment rights
  • No promised or expected returns
  • No obligation to upgrade or participate

About Money, Risk, and Value Growth

Trust ZedFund does not grow money. Any increase in value comes only from real economic activity chosen and managed by members, such as community projects or small businesses.

If a project succeeds, members may benefit. If it fails, losses are possible. This is real life — not a financial product.

Fees and Fairness

Fees are charged for access to tools, education, and transparency — not for access to money or profit.

Paying a membership fee does not make you an investor. It makes you an informed participant or learner.

Your Freedom and Right to Walk Away

You are free to leave Trust ZedFund at any time. We would rather lose a user than push someone into financial decisions they do not fully understand.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Trust ZedFund a bank?

No. Trust ZedFund is not a bank and does not accept deposits.

Is Trust ZedFund an investment fund?

No. We do not invest money on behalf of users or promise returns.

Why do I have to pay if I’m only learning?

Because education, transparency, and systems cost money to maintain. The fee ensures seriousness and sustainability.

Can I lose money?

If you later choose to participate in community projects, yes — losses are possible. Learning alone does not risk your money.

Can I join and never contribute?

Yes. Learning does not require participation.

Who controls pooled funds?

Members and groups control their own funds under agreed rules. Trust ZedFund does not own member funds.

Is participation forced after paying?

No. There is no forced participation or upgrade.

Trust ZedFund operates as a community-focused financial platform under PACRA registration. We prioritize clarity, responsibility, and long-term trust over growth at any cost.
