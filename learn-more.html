Trust ZedFund

A community-powered financial platform for Zambians.

What Trust ZedFund Is

Trust ZedFund helps individuals, groups, and communities organize money responsibly, learn financial discipline, and support real economic activity. We provide financial structure, transparency, education, and coordination — without being a bank or offering regulated investment products.

What Trust ZedFund Is Not

  • Not a bank or microfinance institution
  • Not yet a licensed investment fund
  • Not a profit-guaranteeing platform
  • Not a money-doubling scheme

Why Trust ZedFund Exists

Many people want to save, learn, or support businesses but lack structure, trust, or financial clarity. Trust ZedFund exists to solve this gap by providing a transparent system where people can observe, learn, coordinate, and participate voluntarily.

Membership Types

1. Learner / Observer Membership

Designed for individuals who are starting alone, cautious, or simply want to understand how community finance works before committing money.

  • Access to financial education and practical insights
  • Visibility into how savings circles and pooled funds operate
  • No obligation to contribute money
  • No investment risk

2. Community Saver Membership

For groups such as families, friends, churches, cooperatives, or clubs that want to pool funds responsibly using clear rules and accountability.

  • Group-defined contribution rules
  • Transparent tracking and reporting
  • Funds remain under community control
  • Trust ZedFund provides structure, not decisions

3. Contributor / Venture Support Membership

For individuals or groups who want to support small businesses or community ventures with clear understanding of risk.

  • Access to vetted opportunities
  • Clear documentation and reporting
  • No guaranteed returns
  • Value depends on real economic performance

Trust & Governance

Trust ZedFund operates with full transparency. All decisions are guided by clear rules and shared visibility.

  • Group Decisions: Community Saver and Contributor members vote or agree on how funds are used.
  • Documentation: All contributions, disbursements, and project progress are recorded on the platform.
  • Education & Guidance: Learners are shown examples of how decisions are made, including successes and failures.
  • Transparency: Members can view transactions and outcomes at any time.
  • Dispute Resolution: Clear internal procedures exist if disagreements arise within groups.

Trust ZedFund provides the framework, tools, and education — members provide the trust, discipline, and financial action.
