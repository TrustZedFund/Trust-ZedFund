Our Story

Trust ZedFund was born from a simple observation: while walking through Lusaka's Soweto Market, our founder saw hundreds of hardworking Zambians with incredible business potential, but no access to capital.

Banks required collateral they didn't have. Loan sharks charged predatory rates. Family savings stayed under mattresses, losing value to inflation.

"What if we could connect the savings in our communities with the business opportunities right next door?"

That question became our mission. We're not a foreign fintech company. We're Zambians building solutions for Zambian challenges.
