Mwaba Lewis
Lead Founder — Vision, Capital & Governance

Lewis is the Lead Founder of AfriLeap Capital, an operator-led venture company and studio building foundational technology companies across Africa’s core economic sectors. He focuses on identifying systemic infrastructure gaps, structuring venture theses, and assembling founding teams capable of building from first principles. Lewis leads AfriLeap’s strategy, capital formation, governance, and venture spin-outs, ensuring each company is grounded in real demand, disciplined execution, and long-term value creation. Before AfriLeap, Lewis worked closely with early-stage builders, operators, and SMEs across Africa, with a strong emphasis on fintech, trade, and platform businesses.
